My name is Stuart. I solve problems using data. I currently work within the Business Intelligence division of a consumer packaged goods company, with previous experience as a portfolio analyst for a wealth management firm.

This site is a collection of my thoughts, projects and interests. I’m fascinated by the open source community, and all that can be learned/built from it. I try to work with open source tools such as R as much as I can, and in the spirit of collaboration, share what I learn along the way.

When I’m not crunching numbers, you will most likely find me out on the trail enjoying nature. My ideal day inolves in a challenging hike followed by a gourmet backcountry meal, although I’m still working on the “gourmet” part.

You can find my resume on LinkedIn, my code on Github, or contact me for any questions.
